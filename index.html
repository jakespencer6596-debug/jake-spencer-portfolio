About Me

Photo

I'm a Senior Research & Developer at Akorn Innovations with a track record of building AI-powered web tools that drive measurable conservation impact.

With a B.A. in Economics (Data Science concentration) from Lawrence University and minors in Computer Science and Innovation & Entrepreneurship, I combine technical depth with strategic thinking. My work has contributed to a 400% increase in company revenue, produced 2 pending patents, and powered platforms that have gathered over 150,000 signatures for environmental policy action.

I've partnered with organizations like Pew Charitable Trusts, the Smithsonian, NOAA, and Blue Ocean Conservation to build tools that streamline research, amplify advocacy, and translate data into real-world outcomes.
